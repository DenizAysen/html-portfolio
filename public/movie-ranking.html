The Best Movies According to Deniz

My top 3 movies of all time.

Shawshank Redemption

The story was awesome. Characters written well. I liked the dynamic between main characters.

Dark Knight

Heath Ledger was perfect. He carried the whole movie. Even at the end he won the battle against Batman.

Avengers Infinty War

Visual effects were great. Action never stopped for a moment. Thanos written well. At the Thanos reached his goal.
